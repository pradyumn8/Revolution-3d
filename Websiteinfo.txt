    - Deep Black / Near-Black : Primary text, bold headers, UI elements

    - Electric Yellow / Chartreuse : Used sparingly for CTA hover states,
                                     interactive UI accents, and brand pops

  Design Language:
    - High contrast black-on-white typography
    - Minimal use of saturated colour — when used, it hits hard

    - UI elements rely heavily on motion and typographic contrast rather
      than a colourful palette
